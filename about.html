About Us

MIDORI began as an experimental fashion line catalog-innovative pop art-inspired designs capturing a distinct Tokyo aesthetic. The iconic trademarked motif in particular received media coverage around the world. With apparel created as a canvas to complete the story. MIDORI evolved into designing unisex collections

Jay Yuu Nanasawa is Japanese-Filipino Graphic designer and professional Photographer, same as Lancelot Paclibar who is also a Filipino Fashion designer and Animator. They studied graphic design at Mapua Malayan Colleges Mindanao and graduated in 2023. They are well-known as the creative directors of their brand MIDORI and known for their forte in Davao City. Jay and Lancelot are currently based in Tokyo, Japan.
